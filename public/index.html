Awesome Features

Post Feeds

Browse post feeds from various communities with infinite scroll. Found an intriguing post? Give an upvote or downvote to indicate how useful is the posts. Posts type included text, images and videos

Nested Post Comments

Browse post comments and view other community's opinions on a certain topic. Come with a 2-level nested comment. Able to comment or reply with text and images

Discover Groups

Discover or search various types of groups created at Coteria. There are private and public group types. Join the group to create a post or just browse through the group's posts and topics.

Chat with People

Found like-minded people? Chat with them! Able to send images, replies messages and delete messages. Discuss anything with the people you found intriguing and get to know them.

Notifications

Receive various type of notifications such as when someone comment on your post, reply to your comment, invite you to join a group or someone joined your group and many more!

Create Group or Post

Create your own community with your own rules. Invite members and manage your group. Assign admin to ease the group moderating. Create posts related to group topics including images or videos
